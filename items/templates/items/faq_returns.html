Frequently Asked Questions

✦ How long does it take to craft a customized gift?

Since every item is fully handmade, crafting timelines vary. Standard catalog products take 1-2 days, while highly custom items (like Explosion Boxes or Scrapbooks) require 3-4 days of meticulous design before shipment.

✦ What are the photo size requirements for calendars and frames?

For the 12-Month Calendar and Polaroid Prints, high-resolution square (1:1) or portrait images are best. You can upload them during checkout, or reply to our confirmation email with your files.

✦ How do you ensure fragile gift boxes do not get damaged?

We ship all packages inside double-walled hardboard cartons surrounded by protective bubbles. Each order is labeled as highly fragile and shipped via specialized express air networks to avoid rough sorting hubs.

Return & Refund Policy

We take immense pride in our quality. However, because our products are tailored with personalized pictures, names, and custom elements, we cannot offer standard returns on fully customized items unless there is a clear physical defect or courier transit damage.

Returns Process (Defective Items)

  • Report the damage via email within 48 hours of delivery.
  • Provide snapshots showing the packaging box and the damaged contents.
  • Upon verification, we will craft and ship a brand new replacement, completely free of charge.
Need to file a return claim?

Contact our support team directly with your Order ID to initiate verification.
